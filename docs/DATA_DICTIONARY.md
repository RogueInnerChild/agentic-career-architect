# Career Data Warehouse: Data Dictionary

## Core Objects

### Experience Lake
The master repository of all professional activities, including traditional employment, consulting, and significant projects.

- **persona_alignment**: Used to toggle visibility for different resume versions.
  - `corporate`: Traditional enterprise B2B roles.
  - `startup`: High-growth, ambiguous, or "builder" environments.
  - `ai_innovator`: Focus on agentic workflows and R&D.
- **priority**:
  - `1`: Essential "Hero" bullets.
  - `3`: Supportive detail.
  - `5`: Contextual "filler" or niche wins.

### Narrative Bridges
Designed to explain transitions, sabbaticals, or periods of "R&D" that don't fit a standard job title.
